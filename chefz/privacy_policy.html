Introduction

Chefz Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Chefz KOT - Waiter App and Admin App mobile applications (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Information

We collect the following types of information:

Location Data

  • Purpose: Security and access control
  • Usage: Verify authorized usage locations
  • Permission: Location access

Device Information

  • Purpose: App functionality and security
  • Usage: Device identification and session management
  • Permission: Device state access

Business Data

We collect and store:

  • Menu and pricing information
  • Order and transaction data
  • User accounts and preferences
  • Business settings and configurations

Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage analytics
  • Error reports and performance data
  • Network connectivity status

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Service
  • Managing restaurant operations
  • Processing orders and transactions
  • User authentication and access control
  • Generating business reports
  • Improving app performance
  • Customer support and troubleshooting

Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Storage: Data is stored securely in the cloud
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Secure user authentication and permissions
  • Backup: Regular backups for data recovery
  • Security Measures: Industry-standard security practices

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your restaurant data to third parties.

We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: For hosting and backend services
  • Third-party Services: Only when necessary for app functionality

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Manage app permissions
  • Opt-out of data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@chefz.in

Data Retention

  • Business data is retained as long as you use the Service
  • You can delete your account and data at any time
  • Backup data is retained for 30 days after deletion

Children's Privacy

Our Service is designed for restaurant business operations and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Permissions

Required Permissions:

  • Internet Access: Sync data and provide core functionality
  • Network State: Monitor connectivity status
  • Notifications: App alerts and updates

Optional Permissions:

  • Location: Security and access control (can be denied)
  • Device State: Enhanced functionality (can be denied)
  • Storage: Local data caching (can be denied)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending push notifications for significant changes
  • Email notification to registered admin users

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:
